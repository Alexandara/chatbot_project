Open Lock (Dex; Trained Only)

	Attempting an Open Lock check without a set of thieves’ tools imposes a -2 
circumstance penalty
 on the check, even if a simple tool is employed. If you use masterwork thieves’ tools, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on the check.

Check

	The DC for opening a lock varies from 20 to 40, depending on the quality of the lock, as given on the table below.

	See also: 
epic usages
 of Open Lock.

Action

	Opening a lock is a 
full-round action
.

Special

	If you have the 
Nimble Fingers
 feat, you get a +2 bonus on Open Lock checks.

Untrained

	You cannot pick locks untrained, but you might successfully force them open.
